Drain Services

Clear Drains, Clear Mind

A clogged drain is more than an inconvenience — it can lead to pipe damage, foul odors, and health hazards. Apex Plumbing uses advanced hydro-jetting and mechanical snaking to clear even the toughest blockages in homes and businesses throughout Sanpada, Vashi, Nerul, and Navi Mumbai.

We don't just poke at the problem — we identify the root cause and recommend preventive solutions to keep your drains flowing freely.

  • Kitchen sink and bathroom drain cleaning
  • Main sewer line clearing and inspection
  • Hydro-jetting for grease and mineral buildup
  • Floor drain and storm drain maintenance
  • Camera inspection to locate blockages
  • Preventive maintenance plans available
